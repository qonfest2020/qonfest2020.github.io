Registration

Registration will be soon available using the web-based (through EasyChair) registration form, with online payment on a secure website. Please use one form per attendee.

There are two deadlines for registering to QONFEST, which correspond to different discounts on the registration fees. The deadlines are:

  • Early registration deadline: no later than August 10, 2020
  • Late registration deadline: from August 10 to September 5th, 2020

All deadlines are in Central European Time (CET).
