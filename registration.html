Amid the recent COVID-19 situation, the organization committee decided that QONFEST 2020, will be organized on-line. Accepted papers will be published as planned, by September 2020, but no physical meeting/presentations will take place. We plan that the authors will record their talks and discuss them with the conference participants online.

Registration

Registration is available using the web-based (through EasyChair) registration form, with online payment (only via credit card) on a secure website. Before registering, make sure that you have an EasyChair account and click on the registration link only after having logged in EasyChair. Please use one form per attendee.

There are two deadlines for registering to QONFEST, which correspond to different discounts on the registration fees. The deadlines are:

  • Early registration deadline: no later than August 13th, 2020
  • Late registration deadline: from August 14th to September 5th, 2020

All deadlines are in CEST - Central European Summer Time (Daylight Saving Time).
